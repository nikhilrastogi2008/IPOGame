How it works

Each student gets a notional fund and bids privately — choosing a price within the band and number of lots. When the instructor closes the book, the cut-off price is the price at which the company fully raises its target. Everyone pays that same cut-off price. Unused funds earn interest. A wealth leaderboard reveals the best strategy.

💡 How book building works

The cut-off price is where the company raises its full target. Everyone pays the same cut-off price — not their individual bid. If you bid ₹110 and cut-off is ₹100, you pay ₹100 and get ₹10/share refunded. Bid below cut-off = no allotment. Unused funds earn interest.
